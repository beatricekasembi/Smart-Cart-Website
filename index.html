KEY FEATURES

OFF-THE-SHELF HARDWARE

Built using Radio Frequency Identification (RFID). RFID readers are integrated into any off-the-shelf shopping cart and integrates with the RFID tags that are deployed by the retailer. We envision that our device will be deployed in a setting where all of the items are tagged with unique RFID tags.

SEAMLESS CHECKOUT

The RFID reader continuously interrogates surrounding tags to determine the items have been placed inside the shopping cart. It tracks these in-cart items, and will seamlessly purchase these items once the customer leaves the store. No manual checkout is required.

CUSTOMER INTEREST MONITORING

It also monitors the items that the customer interacts with (e.g., a bottle that is touched or picked up) even if it is not placed into the cart. We observe that the RFID tags on items picked up by the customer have a higher temporal variance in phase and RSSI(Received Signal Strength Indicator) change compared to those untouched items in the vicinit.
